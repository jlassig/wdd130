Overview

Purpose

Create a site where students can see who the advisors of an online university are and what majors they specialize in, what languages they speak, has availability in their time zone, has a short bio about them and then book with them.

Audience

My intended audience are university students who are looking to find the advisor that can best help them with their questions.

Content

Home page

This first page would welcome the student to our site, have them choose a major from a list (probably an unordered list), be able to contact us if their major isn't listed, give them the company motto, have a nav bar and footer. Since I am only doing 3 pages for this project, the only "major" that the student can click on will be Applied Technology. Also, because we are only doing 3 pages, the nav bar would include home, Applied Technology and Book Now. The finished nav bar (outside of class) would not include applied technology, it would have search by advisor (where you could search by the advisor's name you want). Also, all of the major links would take you to a page similar to how Applied Technology is going to look.

HOME PAGE WELCOME: Welcome to Uni Advisors, where we match students up with the advisor who can best help them to navigate their university.

MAJOR SELECTION: Please select your major below, so we can introduce you to our advisors specializing in that major: Applied Technology Applied Business Management Communication Applied Health Marriage and Family Studies Professional Studies

CONTACT US: Contact Us - Don't see your major listed? That's ok we are here to help. Put your information in the text box below and someone will contact you within 7 business days.

COMPANY MOTTO: Uni Advisors - Our job is to help you succeed at your university.

Book Now

The student can get to this page by clicking on the advisor's photo or by clicking on Book Now up at the top nav bar. In the finished (outside of class project), this page would have a drop down with the different advisors names. The student would click on that name and then that advisor's google calendar or calendly scheduler would pop up. Since that would require (I think) javascript, we are going to do it a little different for this class. There will still be a dropdown (Who doesn't love a good drop down?) with the advisors name. The only advisor that the student (in this case) can click on would be Tyler Jensen because the picture of the calendar will be fixed to Tyler Jensen. One day though, I want to be able to add that the student can actually book with the person and any of the people.

Also, I want to include basic instructions for using the calendar below ("Click on the day you want, and then click on the time on the right" "Choose your time zone from the dropdown", etc.)
